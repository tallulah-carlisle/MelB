Purple Owl is the official management company for Melanie Brown MBE

Melanie - aka Mel B - is a global entertainment icon who made her name as one fifth of the world’s biggest selling girl band, The Spice Girls.

Known as Scary Spice to millions of music fans, Mel B has gone on to become a hugely successful television star in shows from America’s Got Talent to The X Factor UK and The X Factor Australia. She has starred on Broadway, in London’s West End and has written a best-selling autobiography, Brutally Honest.

As Patron of Women’s Aid in 2002 she received an MBE for her services to Vulnerable Women. She has spoken at Number 10 Downing Street, the Houses of Parliament and at several business conferences about her campaigning work.
